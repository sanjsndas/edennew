Contact Us

At Eden Drift Gardening, we are dedicated to providing exceptional service and support to our customers. Whether you have questions about our gardening services, want to request a quote, or simply wish to provide feedback, we are here for you. Your experience matters deeply to us, and we strive to respond promptly and helpfully to every inquiry.

1. Customer Service Availability

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM. During these hours, you can reach out to us by phone or email, and we will ensure your queries are addressed with professionalism and care. For urgent matters outside of these hours, please leave a detailed message, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

2. Phone Support

Phone support offers direct access to our knowledgeable representatives who can assist with service inquiries, booking appointments, and provide guidance on our gardening offerings. Call us at +1 (855) 911-7722 to speak with a friendly associate ready to help you make the most of your gardening experience.

3. Email Support

Prefer written communication? Email us at contact@edendriftgardening.com for detailed questions, quotes requests, or to convey any concerns. Our team monitors emails during business hours and aims to respond within one business day. Email is a convenient way to track your communication history and receive clear, written answers.

4. Service Requests

You can request specific gardening or landscaping services by contacting us via phone or email. Provide details about your property, desired services, and preferred scheduling times. Our team will review your request and provide a timely response including pricing and appointment availability.

5. Location and Service Areas

Eden Drift Gardening primarily serves clients in New York City and surrounding areas. Our central office is located at 239 Greene St, New York, NY 10003, USA. We tailor our services based on property location and local environmental conditions, ensuring the best care possible.

6. Request for On-Site Visits

For a comprehensive gardening assessment, you can request an on-site visit by one of our horticulturists or landscapers. During the visit, we evaluate your outdoor space, discuss goals, and offer tailored recommendations. On-site visits help us provide accurate quotes and detailed service plans.

7. Emergency Support

Our team can support certain urgent gardening needs such as storm damage cleanup or pest outbreaks. Though these cases receive priority, availability is subject to schedule and resource allocation. Contact us immediately in such situations and we will do our best to assist quickly.

8. Feedback and Complaints

We welcome your feedback to continually improve our services. If you have concerns or complaints, please contact our customer care team. We commit to resolving issues promptly, professionally, and to your satisfaction. Feedback helps us maintain high standards and client trust.

9. Booking Confirmation

Once a service appointment is requested, we will confirm your booking via email or phone with all relevant details including date, time, and scope of work. Please confirm appointments promptly to ensure scheduling accuracy and avoid conflicts.

10. Payment Inquiries

For questions regarding invoices, payment options, or billing cycles, our accounts team is available to assist. We offer various payment methods including credit card, bank transfer, and cash on delivery where applicable. Payment must be made according to agreed terms to continue uninterrupted service.

11. Requesting Service Changes

If you need to adjust, postpone, or cancel a scheduled service, please notify us at least 24-48 hours in advance. This helps us allocate resources efficiently and offer your slot to other clients as needed. Service changes are subject to availability and cancellation policies.

12. Quotes and Estimates

We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all services based on your requirements and property specifics. It is recommended to provide detailed information or images of your garden to receive the most accurate estimate.

To assist clients with complex gardening projects, we offer personal consultations with experts. These consultations can be in-person or virtual, allowing tailored advice and detailed planning.
