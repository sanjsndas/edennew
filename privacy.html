Privacy Policy

At Eden Drift Gardening, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our services. We are committed to transparency and to safeguarding your data in full compliance with applicable privacy laws.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information you provide voluntarily when using our website or services, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, and payment information. Additionally, we gather information through cookies and analytics to help us understand user behavior and improve our website. This may include data about your browsing habits, clicks, and device information. Our goal is to gather the minimum necessary information required to provide our services and maintain a high-quality experience for you.

2. Use of Personal Information

The primary use of your information is to process your service requests, communicate effectively with you, and customize your experience. We also use personal data for sending service-related updates, promotions, and newsletters in compliance with your consent. Furthermore, we analyze aggregated data to enhance our products and services. Personal information is never sold or rented to third parties, ensuring we prioritize your privacy and trust above all else.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We utilize cookies and similar tracking mechanisms to improve website functionality and offer a personalized user experience. Cookies help us recognize returning visitors, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic patterns. You have control over cookie preferences via your browser settings, which allows you to accept, reject, or remove cookies. However, disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use particular features on our website.

4. Data Security

Protecting your personal data is a top priority. We employ advanced security technologies and procedures, including encryption and secure servers, to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of information. Our internal policies ensure staff members are trained in data protection protocols and access to personal data is restricted strictly to necessary personnel only, minimizing risks.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. When information is no longer needed, we take appropriate steps to securely delete or anonymize it. This practice ensures that your data is not held unnecessarily, maintaining your privacy and reducing risk of data breaches.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with outside parties except for trusted service providers who assist us with essential functions such as payment processing, customer support, and website hosting. These parties are bound by confidentiality agreements and are required to protect your data according to our standards. We also cooperate with law enforcement or regulatory bodies when legally required to disclose information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data held by us. You may also restrict or object to certain processing activities and request data portability where applicable. To exercise your rights, please contact us directly, and we will respond promptly within the legally mandated timeframe. Maintaining transparency and your control over personal data is fundamental to our service.

8. Children’s Privacy

We take special care to protect children’s privacy and do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. Our services and website are intended for general audiences. If we learn that data from a child under 13 was collected in error, we commit to promptly deleting this information to ensure compliance with privacy regulations and to protect young users.

9. International Transfers

Your personal information may be stored and processed in countries outside your own. Wherever such transfers occur, we ensure that adequate security safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws. These safeguards include using standard contractual clauses, adherence to data protection frameworks, or ensuring the destination country has an adequacy decision from relevant authorities.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements, technology, or our operational practices. We encourage visitors to review this page regularly. For significant changes, we will notify you via email or prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

11. How We Use Cookies

Cookies serve essential functions such as enabling website navigation, remembering user preferences, and gathering analytics. We categorize cookies as essential, performance, functionality, and targeting cookies, each serving a specific purpose in enhancing your browsing experience. We respect your privacy choices by providing mechanisms to accept or reject non-essential cookies during your visit.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, our data practices, or wish to exercise data protection rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@edendriftgardening.com or call +1 (855) 911-7722. We are committed to responding to your queries promptly and assisting you in any way we can to protect your privacy.

13. Information You Provide

Information you voluntarily provide enables us to tailor service delivery and improve your customer experience. This includes communications, feedback, survey responses, and booking information. We use this data responsibly and only for purposes directly connected with your relationship with Eden Drift Gardening.

14. Information From Other Sources

Sometimes, we may receive additional information from third-party sources such as public records or marketing partners to corroborate the data you provide, enhance service accuracy, and prevent fraudulent activities. We ensure such data sources comply with privacy regulations before integrating their information into our systems.

15. Purpose Limitation

Your personal data is processed only for clearly stated purposes, such as fulfilling your service requests, marketing with your consent, and complying with legal obligations. We do not use your information for any unrelated purposes without informing you and obtaining your consent where required by law.

16. Legitimate Interests

In specific scenarios, we process data based on our legitimate business interests, such as improving our website’s usability, securing our systems, or protecting against fraud. We carefully balance these interests against your privacy rights and will not proceed if your rights override our interests.

17. Marketing Communications

We communicate relevant gardening news, promotions, and updates through email or other channels pending your consent. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time, ensuring that your preferences for contact are respected without affecting service-related communications.

18. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites for your convenience. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. We advise you to review their privacy statements before providing any personal information to those sites.

19. Data Controller

Eden Drift Gardening is the data controller who determines the purposes and means of processing your personal data. Our commitment is to comply with all applicable privacy laws and protect the data entrusted to us.

20. Legal Basis for Processing

Our processing of your data relies on lawful bases such as your consent, contractual necessity, legal obligations, and legitimate interests. We ensure these bases are appropriate for each processing activity we undertake concerning your personal information.

21. Data Protection Principles

We strictly follow principles of lawful processing, data minimization, accuracy, storage limitation, integrity, and confidentiality. These principles guide us in handling your data responsibly and ensuring transparency about our practices.

22. Rights to Access and Portability

You have the right to request access to your personal data, receive a copy of the information held by us, and request transfer of your data to another controller in a machine-readable format. This empowers you to maintain control over your personal information.

23. Rectification and Erasure

If your personal data is inaccurate or incomplete, you can request correction. Additionally, under certain circumstances, you have the right to request deletion of your data, especially when it is no longer necessary for the purposes it was collected.

24. Restriction of Processing

You may request the restriction of processing your data under specific situations, such as when accuracy is disputed or processing is unlawful but you prefer restriction instead of erasure. We will honor such requests in accordance with applicable laws.

25. Objection to Processing

You have the right to object to data processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes. Upon objection, we will either stop processing your data or demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds overriding your interests.

26. Automated Decision-Making

We do not engage in automated decision-making or profiling that leads to legal effects or similarly significant impacts on you without explicit consent. Any decisions impacting your services will involve human review and oversight.

27. Security Measures

We use industry-standard security technologies including encryption, firewalls, and secure access protocols to protect personal data and minimize risks. Regular audits and employee training ensure ongoing vigilance and compliance with security best practices.

28. Data Breach Procedures

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we have established procedures to identify, contain, and assess the breach. We notify affected individuals and relevant authorities promptly in accordance with legal obligations to minimize harm.

29. International Transfers

When transferring your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses or reliance on adequacy decisions are in place to maintain a robust level of data protection in the destination countries.

30. Cookies and Tracking Technologies Overview

We categorize cookies as essential for website functionality, performance cookies for analytics, functionality cookies for enhanced experience, and advertising cookies for tailored content. Your preferences are respected with options to manage or disable non-essential cookies.

31. Managing Cookie Preferences

You have control over cookie settings via browser controls or cookie management tools on our website, allowing you to accept, decline, or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the usability and features of the website.

32. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not designed for children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect children's data. Should we discover personal data of a child collected unknowingly, we will promptly delete it to protect their privacy and comply with legal requirements.

33. Data Protection Officer

Our Data Protection Officer oversees the implementation and compliance of this Privacy Policy. They serve as the contact point for privacy concerns or questions. You can reach them at privacy@edendriftgardening.com for support.

34. User Responsibilities

We rely on users to provide accurate and truthful information and to protect the confidentiality of any login credentials or devices used for accessing our services. Notify us immediately of any unauthorized use or security breaches concerning your data.

35. Consent Withdrawal

If you have provided consent for certain data processing activities, you have the right to withdraw this consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of earlier processing and will be handled in compliance with legal requirements.

36. Legal Compliance

Eden Drift Gardening complies with all relevant data protection legislation, including GDPR where applicable. We cooperate with data protection authorities and comply with lawful requests or investigations concerning data protection matters.

37. Third-Party Service Providers

We engage with third-party service providers only after verifying their compliance with strong data protection practices. Contracts require adherence to confidentiality and data security obligations to safeguard your personal information.

38. Email Communications

Marketing and service-related communications are provided in accordance with your preferences and applicable laws. You have the right to unsubscribe or opt out at any time through provided links or by contacting us directly, ensuring control over your communication preferences.

39. Modifications to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or technological advances. Changes will be posted on this page with the effective date updated. Significant amendments will be communicated additionally to keep you informed.

40. Contacting Us

Questions, complaints, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data can be directed to our dedicated privacy team at privacy@edendriftgardening.com. You may also reach us by phone at +1 (855) 911-7722. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and thoroughly to maintain your trust.
